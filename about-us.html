Our Structured Pathways

At the heart of Leapnest Academy lies our Placement Training Program, designed to equip college students with the critical skills needed to excel in competitive recruitment environments. We offer structured modules in:

Aptitude & Logical Reasoning

Quantitative aptitude, analytical reasoning logic, speed math shortcuts, and mock problem-solving scenarios.

Verbal & Communication Skills

Vocabulary enrichment, correct grammar usage, reading comprehension, and professional business english speaking.
